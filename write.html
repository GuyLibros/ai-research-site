🖊️ Writing and Revising with AI

AI can support your writing process — from organizing rough ideas to polishing your final draft — but it works best when paired with your own voice, judgment, and critical thinking.

✍️ How AI Can Help with Writing

  • Generate topic sentences or introductory paragraphs
  • Rephrase awkward sentences or improve clarity
  • Help develop transitions between sections
  • Offer grammar, tone, or sentence structure suggestions

📋 Prompt Examples for Writing

"Write an introduction for a paper about how geography shaped rural identity in 19th-century Western Maryland."
"Give me 3 topic sentences for paragraphs in a paper about environmental justice."
"Improve the clarity and flow of this paragraph: [paste your paragraph here]"
"Suggest transitions between these two sections: [paste text here]"

⚠️ Use AI Thoughtfully

  • Don't copy full responses into your paper without editing — AI doesn’t cite sources or know your exact assignment.
  • Always review AI output for factual accuracy and tone.
  • Use AI to support revision — not to replace writing and reflection.

🎓 Academic Integrity Reminder

If you use AI to help generate, revise, or refine your writing, be transparent if required by your instructor and always double-check your school’s policies on AI use.
