🔄 AI Research Workflow & Prompt Templates

Use this guide to see how AI can support you through each stage of the research process.

📊 Research Workflow: From Question to Draft

  1. Brainstorm Topics
    "Give me 5 academic research questions about [broad topic]."
  2. Refine Research Question
    "Help me narrow this topic: [insert question]."
  3. Search Strategy
    "Suggest search keywords and Boolean phrases for this question."
  4. Find & Summarize Sources
    "Summarize this article in plain English: [paste text]."
  5. Analyze Evidence
    "What is the author's main argument in this passage?"
  6. Generate an Outline
    "Create a 5-part outline for a paper on [your question]."
  7. Draft & Revise
    "Can you help me improve the clarity of this paragraph: [paste]."
  8. Cite AI or Ask About Ethics
    "How do I cite ChatGPT in MLA style?"

📋 Prompt Templates for Each Stage

  • 🧠 Topic Brainstorming: "Suggest interesting research angles on [topic]."
  • 🛠️ Research Question Refinement: "Turn this into a researchable question: [idea]."
  • 🔎 Source Discovery: "What kinds of primary sources could help me answer [question]?"
  • 📖 Summarization: "Summarize this article in bullet points."
  • 🧠 Analysis: "What does the author mean in this quote?"
  • 📝 Outline & Draft: "Suggest a paper structure for this thesis: [thesis]."
  • 🧹 Revision: "Make this paragraph more concise without changing the meaning."
  • ⚖️ Academic Integrity: "What are best practices for using AI in academic writing?"
