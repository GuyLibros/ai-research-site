🧾 Analyze and Summarize Texts with AI

Once you begin reading your sources, AI tools can help you digest, interpret, and organize the content more effectively—especially when dealing with historical texts, scholarly articles, or dense academic writing.

How AI Can Help

  • Summarize long articles or historical documents to extract the key points quickly
  • Explain complex vocabulary or dated language—great for 19th-century journals or academic prose
  • Highlight main arguments and supporting evidence for easier note-taking and analysis

Example Prompts

“Summarize this passage from a 19th-century travel journal in plain language.”
“Identify the author’s main argument in this abstract.”
“What evidence does the author use to support their claim?”
“Explain this quote in simpler terms for a first-year college student.”

Recommended Tools

  • ChatGPT: Great for summarizing, explaining, or paraphrasing content
  • Claude (Anthropic): More conversational tone, strong at interpreting long documents
  • Perplexity.ai: Useful for summary + source checking
