Getting Started with Generative AI in Academic Research

Start with a broad idea—or no idea at all—and let AI help you shape a research direction that fits your course goals.

Welcome

This guide is intended for students seeking to integrate generative AI tools—such as ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Poe—into the academic research process. Our emphasis is on responsible use and critical engagement.
