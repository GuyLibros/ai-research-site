📝 Outline and Structure Your Paper

Use AI to organize your ideas, create structure, and transition from question to thesis to paper.

🧠 Why Use AI for Outlining?

Outlining can feel overwhelming, but AI can help break it down. Whether you’re building a traditional 5-paragraph essay or a longer research paper, AI can guide you through the structure and logic of your argument.

✅ What AI Can Do

  • Suggest paper structures based on your research question or thesis
  • Generate section headers and transitions
  • Compare thematic angles (economic, social, cultural, environmental, etc.)
  • Translate a question into a thesis and outline key supporting ideas

💬 Example Prompts

Use these prompts to get started with your outline in an AI tool like ChatGPT or Claude:

  • "Based on my research question, suggest a 5-paragraph outline with topic sentences."
  • "Compare cultural vs. economic influences on rural Appalachian identity in the 1800s. How could I structure an essay around this?"
  • "Turn this research question into a thesis and outline: How did Appalachian geography shape rural identity in 19th-century Western Maryland?"

📋 Sample Output

"Based on the question, suggest a 5-paragraph outline with topic sentences."

  1. Introduction: Appalachian geography played a central role in shaping the cultural and social identity of rural Western Maryland during the 19th century.
  2. Paragraph 1: The region’s mountainous landscape created geographic isolation that reinforced tight-knit rural communities.
  3. Paragraph 2: Isolation also shaped unique religious and cultural practices distinct from urban and coastal Maryland.
  4. Paragraph 3: Economic opportunities, such as coal mining and subsistence farming, were shaped by the land and influenced rural identity.
  5. Conclusion: Geography was more than background — it was a defining force in shaping identity, tradition, and daily life.
