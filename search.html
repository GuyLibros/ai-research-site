🧠 Build a Search Strategy with AI

Use AI to turn your research question into effective keywords and database search strategies.

🔍 Why Search Strategy Matters

Once you have a research question, finding academic sources requires turning your idea into strategic keywords and phrases. AI tools can help you generate and test effective search strategies quickly and clearly.

✅ 1. Turn Your Question into Keywords

AI can identify the main concepts in your research question and suggest synonyms or related terms.

Prompt: 
"My research question is: How did Appalachian geography shape rural identity in 19th-century Western Maryland? Suggest keywords and related terms I can use in library databases."

💡 Example output:

  • Appalachian Mountains
  • Rural identity / community culture
  • 19th century / 1800s
  • Western Maryland / Allegany County / Garrett County

✅ 2. Generate Boolean Search Phrases

AI can help combine your keywords using AND, OR, NOT to create advanced searches in databases.

Prompt: 
"Using these keywords, generate a Boolean search phrase I can use in JSTOR or Academic Search Complete."

💡 Example output:

("Appalachian Mountains" OR "Western Maryland") AND ("rural identity" OR "community life") AND ("19th century" OR 1800s)

✅ 3. Identify Subject Terms

Most academic databases use controlled vocabulary (subject headings). AI can help you identify these terms based on your topic, or suggest where to find them.

Prompt: 
"What are common subject headings used in academic databases for my research topic?"

💡 Example output:

  • Appalachian Region — Social Life and Customs
  • Geography and culture
  • Rural Sociology — Maryland
  • Maryland, Western — History — 19th century

🧰 Tools to Try

  • ChatGPT — Turn research questions into keywords and Boolean strings
  • Claude — Offer variations and filtering strategies
  • Perplexity.ai — Suggest keywords and cite where they're used
  • Library of Congress Subject Headings (LCSH) — Explore official subject terms
